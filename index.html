Welcome to The Art Auction Game:

The rules are simple. The art auction will start up with different artpieces that you definitely want to exhibit in your museum. You can bid on the artpieces, but you don't want to over pay for them. There is a price card near the stage that is the correct price to pay for each piece.

Below the stage are four notorious art critics who are here to contribute money to the cause. Ask (click) one of them and they will be willing to prive you some money. However, they will not tell you how much money they are helping you with, so you will have to determine how much the critic is willing to give you for a particular piece. You can ask a critic multiple times and they will give you more money.

With the critics' money, you need to match the price on the price card. If you go over the amount, then your critics will be unhappy and will leave you to pay for everything on your own in which case, you will not be able to keep the artpiece.

So choose wisely and have fun collecting for your museum.
